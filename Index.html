It was in 2008 on the carpets of the Nova Uniao in Rio de Janeiro that Eric obtained from the hands of Yan Cabral his black belt and has since devoted himself to training the future generations of china jiu jitsu champions. , a team of passionate professionals quickly formed around him to participate in the development of the Aranha Team in China.
Reflecting the career direction of Yan Cabral, who has decided to move towards the MMA in recent years, MMA or bjj courses are also open in Guangzhou, The goal is to disclose and make accessible an increasingly widespread practice. With the help of Yan Cabral now set in Barcelona, ​​and thanks to the talents of the Chinese team, the Aranha Association is now a pole of jiu Brazilian jitsu in China, notably with the organization of events around BJJ, competitions or other technical courses, but also by developing a training project for the frameworks around the discipline (referees and teachers).
